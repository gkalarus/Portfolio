About me

Hi, I'm Grzegorz! I'm a 28-year-old engineer who graduated in 2014 from AGH University of Science and Technology with Master of Science degree in Environmental Engineering. For almost 2 years I was professionally involved in the construction industry, working as a mechanical engineer (HVAC systems) and for several months I worked as a logistics specialist in a production company. I have recently been fascinated by the web design (front-end technologies) and since then I have been constantly deepening my knowledge in this area. My current career goal is to find a job in a company that will enable me to deepen my interests, passions and skills related to the web development.
